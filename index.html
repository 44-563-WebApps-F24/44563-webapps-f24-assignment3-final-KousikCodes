New York City

New York City is my favorite for its unmatched energy, cultural diversity, and endless things to explore.
From iconic landmarks to vibrant neighborhoods, it’s a place full of inspiration and excitement at every turn.

In New York City, I’d take a visitor to (Ordered List):

  1. Central Park
  2. Empire State Building
  3. Time's Square

Places near NYC that are outside the city, I'd like to visit: (unordered list):

  • Bear Mountain State Park
    • Scenic hiking trails with stunning views of the Hudson River and surrounding mountains.
    • Perfect for picnicking, boating, and seasonal outdoor activities like ice skating.
  • Storm King Art Center
  • Coney Island
